RYAN AUSTIN

Hi, I’m Ryan Austin! I’m a:

  • Technical Sound Designer,
  • Composer, Sound Designer,
  • Producer, Audio Implementer,
  • Multi-Instrumentalist, Vocalist,
  • Programmer, Audio Enthusiast

I’m passionate about all things audio—building interactive audio systems for games, designing sounds for media, composing diverse styles of music, and performing live. I thrive at the intersection of technology and creativity, specializing in interactive audio and game development.

I graduated from Berklee College of Music in August 2024 with a degree in Game and Interactive Media Scoring, where I discovered my passion for interactive audio and technical sound design. I’m Wwise 101, 201, 251, and 301 certified, with hands-on experience in game audio implementation, sound design, and audio programming. My work includes creating immersive audio systems for projects like Flighting Game, which you can download here on my website.

As a multi-instrumentalist and producer, I play guitar, piano, bass, and drums, combining my musical expertise with my technical skills to craft compelling audio experiences. I’m passionate about pushing the boundaries of interactive audio in games, using sound to enhance storytelling and create unforgettable player experiences.
