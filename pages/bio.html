About Me

Hi! I'm Ryan Austin. I'm a Sound Designer, Audio Programmer, Composer, Technical Sound Designer, and Musician. I specialize in building interactive audio systems for games, designing sounds for media, and composing music in various styles. After graduating from Berklee College of Music with a degree in Game and Interactive Media Scoring, I developed a passion for interactive audio and technical sound design. I'm Wwise certified and have hands-on experience in game audio implementation, sound design, and audio programming, which you can explore through the video projects featured on my website!
